## How it works

### Set it up once

Add your basics once and let the app do the thinking in the background.

### Train to your energy

Get a session that fits the day you are actually having, with easier fallback options when energy is low and fuller sessions when you have more in the tank.

### Keep the streak alive

Build consistency with smart nudges, quick wins, daily dopamine, and less admin.
