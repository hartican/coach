Dynamic prep

5-minute warmup

Move continuously. Aim to raise temperature, open the hips and shoulders, and prepare your trunk for planks and loaded patterns.

  • 1 minute: March in place with band pull-aparts.
  • 1 minute: Goblet squat with a light dumbbell, controlled tempo.
  • 1 minute: Alternating reverse lunge with overhead reach or light band tension.
  • 1 minute: Walk-out to high plank, then stand and perform 4-6 band rows.
  • 1 minute: Easy rope skip, fast march, or band-resisted march.

Warmup cues

  • Keep the first minute easy and rhythmic rather than explosive.
  • Use a light load only; the goal is activation, not fatigue.
  • On the walk-out, brace your ribs down before stepping into plank.
  • If skipping feels awkward, swap for a fast march or low pogo hops.
Main session

20-minute circuit

Complete 3 rounds. Rest 20-30 seconds between exercises and 60-90 seconds between rounds.
